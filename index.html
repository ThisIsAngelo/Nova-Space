PLANET

Planet or planetesimal is a celestial body orbiting a star or stellar remnant, possessing its own gravity, not undergoing thermonuclear fusion, and clearing its orbital zone of planetesimals.

Earth

Earth ranks as the third nearest planet to the Sun and stands as both the densest and fifth largest among the eight planets in the Solar System. As part of the four terrestrial planets, Earth boasts a diameter of approximately 12,742 kilometers and maintains an average temperature hovering around 15 degrees Celsius. Recognized as the "Blue Planet", it earns this moniker due to its unique appearance when observed from the depths of space.

  • Name: Earth
  • Position in the Solar System: Third from the Sun
  • Size: Diameter is approximately 12,742 kilometers.
  • Average Temperature: Approximately 15 degrees Celsius
  • Rotation Speed: Earth rotates on its axis at a speed of about 1,670 kilometers per hour
